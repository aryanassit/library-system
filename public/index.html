About BMS

The Book Management System (BMS) is a passion project designed for the modern reader. We believe that managing your book collection should be as enjoyable as reading itself. Our platform provides intuitive tools to catalog your library, track reading habits, and connect with a community of fellow book lovers, all within a beautiful, user-friendly interface.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is BMS?

BMS (Book Management System) is a modern web application designed to help you organize and manage your digital library effortlessly. It provides tools for cataloging books, tracking reading progress, and discovering new reads.

Is BMS free to use?

Basic features are free to use. However, premium features such as advanced analytics, cloud backup, and community features may require a subscription or one-time payment for verification.

How do I add books to my library?

You can add books manually by entering details or use our ISBN scanner feature. Simply navigate to the book entry section and follow the prompts to add your books to your collection.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All user data is encrypted and stored securely. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for more details.

What browsers are supported?

BMS works best with modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend keeping your browser updated for the best experience.

How do I contact support?

You can reach our support team through the contact form on this page, or by emailing support@bms-library.com. We're here to help with any questions or issues you may have.

Can I export my book data?

Yes, premium users can export their book data in various formats including CSV, JSON, and PDF. This feature allows you to backup your data or migrate to other systems if needed.

Is there a mobile app?

Currently, BMS is a web-based application optimized for mobile browsers. We're working on dedicated mobile apps for iOS and Android, which will be available in the near future.
